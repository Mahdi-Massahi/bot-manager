How to terminate a running strategy?


1. Check running strategies by the command: "nebixbot -shr"


2. Copy unique id of the strategy you want to terminate (the strategy id is in
    bold font)


3. Use "nebixbot -t <strategy id>" to terminate the desired strategy. (
    <strategy id> is your given unique strategy id)


4. Wait for to clean up and terminate your strategy process and eventually
    it will shows either a success or fail message


5. Has it been successful you can check the terminated strategies by:
    "nebixbot -sht" and look for the previously given unique strategy id
