How to terminate a running bot?

1. Check running bots by the command: "nebixbm -shr"
2. Copy unique id of the bot you want to terminate (the bot id is in
    bold font)
3. Use "nebixbm -t <bot id>" to terminate the desired bot. (
    <bot id> is your given unique bot id)
4. Wait for to clean up and terminate your bot process and eventually
    it will shows either a success or fail message
5. Has it been successful you can check the terminated bots by:
    "nebixbm -sht" and look for the previously given unique bot id
